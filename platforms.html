Known Platforms¶

This section provides a list of known values for various Platform (Object) attributes.

Unless otherwise specified, platform attributes are case insensitive (use of lower case is preferred).

Instruction Sets¶

  • IA-32 family: i386, i486, i586, i686
  • Other Intel-derived: ia-64, x86_64[1]
  • ARM family: armel, armeb, armhf
  • Others: alpha, hppa, m68k, mips, mipsel, powerpc, ppc64, sparc
[1]Do not use amd64.
